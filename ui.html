UI/UX Design Brief

Design for the New Norm that will Evolve in Post Covid-19 World.

Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella said in a talk,

" As COVID-19 impacts every aspect of our work and life, we have seen 2 years' worth of digital transformation in 2 months. From remote teamwork and to sales and customer service to critical cloud infrastructure and security, we are working alongside customers every day to help them stay open for business in a world of remote everything ".

Yes, it means a New Normal is going to evolve and it requires new artefacts to adapt to it. As Digital shift is being expected in every sectors from Hospitality, Travel, Shopping, Production to Health care, and there is a need to build design solutions for the new normal.

Deliverables

A Proposal Contains a Design Document with a Proper Prototype.

The design document should deliver information on the Problem identification, Solutions, User Study and Analysis, User Scenarios, Wireframes, User Flow and Experience Factors.
